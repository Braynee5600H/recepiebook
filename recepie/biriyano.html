Biriyani

Ingredients

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500g chicken or mutton
  • 2 large onions (sliced)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 1 cup yogurt
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1/2 cup fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 1 tsp saffron (optional)
  • Salt to taste
  • Oil and ghee for cooking

Instructions

1. Cook rice with whole spices until 70% cooked, drain water and set aside.

2. Fry sliced onions in oil and set them aside for garnish.

3. Marinate chicken/mutton with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, and salt.

4. Cook marinated chicken/mutton with tomatoes and spices until tender.

5. Layer rice and meat, sprinkle saffron milk, mint, and coriander. Cook on low heat for 15 minutes.

6. Serve hot with raita or salad.
