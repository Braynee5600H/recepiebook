Butter Chicken

Ingredients

  • 500g chicken (boneless)
  • 2 cups tomato puree
  • 1 cup cream
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Instructions

1. Marinate chicken with yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, garam masala, and salt for 1 hour.

2. Cook chicken in a pan with butter until golden brown.

3. Prepare the sauce by cooking tomato puree, butter, cream, and spices.

4. Add the cooked chicken to the sauce and simmer for 10-15 minutes.

5. Garnish with cilantro and serve with naan or rice.
