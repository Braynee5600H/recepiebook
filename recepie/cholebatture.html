Chole Bhature

Ingredients for Chole

  • 2 cups dried chickpeas (soaked overnight)
  • 1 large onion (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 2 tbsp chole masala powder
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp oil

Ingredients for Bhature

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 tbsp semolina (rava)
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp yogurt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • Salt to taste
  • Warm water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

1. In a pressure cooker, cook soaked chickpeas until soft.

2. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds, chopped onions, and green chilies. Fry until onions turn golden.

3. Add ginger-garlic paste, pureed tomatoes, and cook for 5-7 minutes.

4. Add spices (turmeric, coriander powder, chole masala), and salt. Add cooked chickpeas, simmer for 15 minutes.

5. For the bhature, prepare dough, rest for 1 hour, roll out and fry in hot oil until puffed.

6. Serve hot chole with bhature, garnish with cilantro.
