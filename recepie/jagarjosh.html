Jagarjoosh (Mutton Curry)

Ingredients

  • 1 kg mutton (bone-in pieces)
  • 2 large onions (finely chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (pureed)
  • 2 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2-3 green chilies (slit)
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp coriander powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2-3 cloves
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Water as needed

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pot, add cumin seeds, cloves, and cinnamon stick. Fry for a minute.

2. Add chopped onions and fry until golden brown.

3. Add ginger-garlic paste and green chilies, cook for another minute.

4. Add pureed tomatoes, red chili powder, coriander powder, and salt. Cook until the oil separates.

5. Add mutton pieces, sear them for a few minutes, then add enough water to cover the meat. Cook on low heat for 1.5 to 2 hours until tender.

6. Sprinkle garam masala and garnish with chopped cilantro. Serve with naan or rice.
