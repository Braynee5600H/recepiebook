Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • 1 cup milk powder
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 tsp baking soda
  • 2 tbsp ghee
  • 1/4 cup warm milk
  • Sugar for syrup
  • Water for syrup
  • Cardamom powder
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

1. Mix milk powder, flour, and baking soda. Add ghee and warm milk, knead into a soft dough.

2. Make small balls from the dough. Heat oil in a pan and fry the balls until golden brown.

3. Prepare sugar syrup with water, sugar, and cardamom. Dip the fried jamuns in warm syrup.

4. Serve the sweet, syrup-soaked gulab jamuns hot or at room temperature.
