Masala Dosa

Ingredients

  • 2 cups rice
  • 1 cup urad dal
  • Potato filling (boiled potatoes, spices)
  • Oil for cooking

Instructions

1. Soak rice and dal, then grind into a batter.

2. Ferment the batter overnight.

3. Spread the batter on a hot pan to make a thin dosa.

4. Add potato filling and fold the dosa.

5. Serve hot with chutney and sambar.
