Paneer Tikka

Ingredients

  • 250g paneer (cubed)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp red chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish

Instructions

1. In a bowl, mix yogurt, spices, lemon juice, and oil to make a marinade.

2. Add paneer cubes to the marinade and let it marinate for 1 hour.

3. Preheat the grill or oven. Skewer the paneer and grill for 10-12 minutes, brushing with oil.

4. Serve hot, garnished with coriander leaves, and serve with chutney.
