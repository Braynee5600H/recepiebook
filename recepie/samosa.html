Samosa

Ingredients for Filling

  • 2 cups boiled potatoes (mashed)
  • 1 cup boiled peas
  • 1 medium onion (finely chopped)
  • 2-3 green chilies (finely chopped)
  • 1 tsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 tsp garam masala powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • 1 tbsp oil

Ingredients for Dough

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1/2 tsp ajwain (carom seeds)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • Oil for deep frying

Instructions

1. Heat oil in a pan, add cumin seeds and chopped onions. Fry until golden.

2. Add ginger-garlic paste, chopped green chilies, and cook for a minute.

3. Add spices, peas, and mashed potatoes. Stir and cook for 5-7 minutes. Garnish with cilantro.

4. For the dough, mix flour, ajwain, salt, and oil. Gradually add water to form a stiff dough. Let rest for 30 minutes.

5. Roll the dough into small balls, roll out into circles, and fill with the potato mixture. Seal into cones.

6. Fry the samosas in hot oil until golden and crispy. Serve with chutneys.
